      Hit Me Up

    You can call me a lot of things. A creative, a builder, a musician, a writer, a story-teller, etc, but you are most likely here because you needed something done on the web. That's where I am right now. The road that I took to get here has been fucking crazy. I wouldn't have it any other way.

    In one life I was a professional musician (i'm still pretty active), in another I earned more than $2 million dollars competing in professional poker tournaments. But despite being successful it didn't scratch my need to build, create, and do something positive. I decided I needed to put my creativity and desire to good use and pursue something different. I joined Knurture, a boutique UI/UX firm based in Durham, NC who design and build interfaces for some of the brightest startups in the world. I soaked up all of the knowledge available. I learned first hand the value of design thinking and user-centric building practices. I had the opportunity to learn and study everything from business development and sales to wireframing, user research, and UI/UX design principles. I also managed a small team inhouse. My time there was invaluable.

    Ultimately, I knew I wanted to build my own thing and knew I needed better technical skills in order to be successful. I began teaching myself to code. As luck would have it I found out that the Iron Yard needed a teacher's assistant for an upcoming front end class. It was a perfect fit. Later, I joined Groundwork Labs as their first ever developer-in-residence and had the opportunity to experience a startup accelerator through the eyes of both a founder and a developer.

    Now, I am help design, brand, market, develop, and ultimately grow digital products for companies and brands. Get in touch.

    If you have a project I would love to hear from you (tomrau@gmail.com). I'm always up for a challenge. Or just say hi/follow me on the social media machine.
